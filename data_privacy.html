Privacy Policy for Dog's Dice

Last Updated: 2024-11-20

At Dog's Dice, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile game. By creating an account and using the app, you agree to the terms of this policy.


1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address. Note: account creation is not mandatory for using the app.

  • Other Information: Other personal information might be shared with third parties for the purpose of advertising. See 3. Advertising below for further guidance.


2. How We Use Your Data

If you create an account we use your email address for the following purposes:

  • To enable you to log into your account from multiple devices or after reinstalling the app.

  • To improve your user experience within the app by allowing account management.

  • To ensure that we can contact you in the event of account issues or updates.


3. Advertising

We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to serve advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect certain information from your device, such as:

  • Device and app identifiers

  • App usage data

  • Location data (depending on your device settings)

All ads have child-directed setting, which means no personalized ads will be shown.

For more information how Admob treats your data, please see Google's Privacy & Terms.


4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for up to 3 years after your last login. After this period, your data will be deleted from our systems. You can also request the deletion of your data at any time by using the account management tools within the app.


5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement industry-standard measures, including:

  • Encrypted communication between the app and our servers.

  • Secure servers to store your data.

  • Encryption of sensitive data such as account credentials.

Despite these measures, no system can guarantee complete security. We work hard to protect your information but cannot guarantee the security of your data.


6. User Rights

As a user of Dog's Dice, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can access your account information at any time within the app.

  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and personal data at any time via the app.

    • Follow these steps to delete your account: Link

  • Restrictions: Once you create an account, you cannot change your email address within the app.


7. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), respectively:

  • Right to Access: You can request to view the personal data we have about you.

  • Right to Rectification: If any information we hold about you is inaccurate, you can request correction.

  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data.

  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to certain types of data processing.

  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of the personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

  • No Sale of Personal Data: We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

To exercise these rights, contact us at dogs-dice@meshing-pumpkins.de.


8. Children's Privacy

Dog's Dice is accessible to users of all ages, including children under 13. We collect the same minimal information (email address) from all users, regardless of age, and no additional personal or sensitive data is collected from children. If a parent or guardian believes that their child has provided us with personal information without their consent, they should contact us at dogs-dice@meshing-pumpkins.de, and we will promptly delete the information.


9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be communicated through the app, and we encourage you to review this policy regularly.


10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at dogs-dice@meshing-pumpkins.de.

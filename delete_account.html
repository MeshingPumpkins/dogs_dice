Delete your Dog's Dice Account

Last Updated: 2024-10-01

  1. Login into the app with your accout that you want to delete.

  2. On the main screen go the the settings page (gear wheel icon on the bottom left) and select „Delete Account“.

  3. In the further process you will be required to enter you password again and confirm the deletion.

  4. After final confirmation your account will be deleted immediatelly.

  5. Your username and statistics may be visible in the leaderboard for a certain period after deletion.

If you fail to log into your account, don’t remember your password or wish to also delete your username and statistics immediately, please contact us at info@meshing-pumpkins.de.
